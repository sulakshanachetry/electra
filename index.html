KEYWORDS OF ELECTRA SOCIETY

The keywords of the Electra Society are as follows:

Idea

It requires a small idea to start for something big. Electra Society is open for any productive idea from its fellow members and it implements every feasible idea in its events and activities that it conducts.

Concept

The objective of the society is the overall development of its students. Be it technical or non-technical, it aims at making its students efficient and capable enough to face any problems in the real world.

Teamwork

Team efforts makes the impossible possible. It is the backbone of Electra Society. Every member contribute in the best possible way to achieve the set of predefined goals.

Development

A good leadership and sense of responsibility is very important for the smooth running of a team. Under the proper guidance of able seniors, the juniors here are guided towards a better future.
